My process

Every product I help create is guided by the needs and objectives of each customer. Rather it be a lesson on overcoming adversity or a web application to attract users, every deliverable is directed by client needs and uncompromised values.

Design

Assess the project and determine client design needs. I use time proven principles, such as the ADDIE model, to ensure each design exceeds industry standards.

Develop

Orient to the options and develop for success. Incremental and iterative development helps ensure that each component fits the desired look, feel, and functionality.

Deliver

On budget and on time. I work with clients to implement the right product for their needs. Training and final customiziations help to solidify the desired outcome.

Resilient and Sustainable

Each project I work on is geared to be enduring and maintainable. I want each client to be able to manage and build upon the work we do together as needed. That means helping partners to undertsand the product and its employment so they may fully realize the potential of what we build together. Contact me to find out more about my process and products.
