About Me

Enabling others to build their own fire no matter the storm.

D.A. Gregory

Educator, Instructional Designer, Web Developer

Originally from a small Texas town just west of Houston, I am drawn to solving practical problems, exploring, and enabling the growth of others. Those drivers served me well for 20 years as a USAF Survival, Evasion, Resistance, and Escape Specialist, responsible for enabling others to survive and "return with honor". As a SERE Specialist I became interested in refining our training products and systems, while developing new ones that best met the needs of our programs and clients. Due to the diverse range of students I had, and the wildly varying climates I operated in, it was of great benefit to develop teaching tools and techniques that were modern yet embraced time tested motivation and behavior principles. The results enabled training success from the wilds of Alaska to the jungles of Central America, through the briefing rooms of commanding generals and training rooms throughout the land. All made possible by employing the right tools, with the best techniques, using world class content.

I have a lifelong passion for games and technology and embrace new and established trends where effective. That passion made web technologies a natural fit , both for the enjoyment of it, and to solve practical problems for myself and my clients. I realized long ago that the customer and the content matter together. Every product I deliver prioritizes the integrity of the content in synchrony with the positive experience of the learner.
